# Business Requirements Document

# Project

RiskOps AI Platform

---

# Business Problem

Risk Operations teams receive millions of transactions every day from multiple payment channels.

Analysts spend significant time reconciling revenue, identifying suspicious activity, investigating chargebacks and preparing executive reports.

Data is often distributed across multiple operational systems, making investigations slow and inconsistent.

---

# Business Objective

Create a centralized analytics platform that enables Risk Operations teams to:

- Monitor transaction health
- Detect operational anomalies
- Reconcile daily revenue
- Analyze chargeback trends
- Measure business KPIs
- Accelerate investigations using AI-assisted insights

---

| Department | Business Need |

# Key Performance Indicators

- Daily Transaction Volume
- Transaction Success Rate

- Chargeback Rate

# Success Criteria

- Single source of operational truth
- Automated daily reporting
- AI-assisted anomaly explanation
