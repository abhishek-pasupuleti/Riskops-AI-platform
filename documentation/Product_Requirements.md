# Product Requirements Document

## Product

RiskOps AI Platform

---

## Purpose

RiskOps AI Platform is an internal analytics solution that helps Risk Operations teams monitor transactions, reconcile revenue, investigate chargebacks and generate executive reports from a unified analytics platform.

---

## Users

- Risk Operations Analysts
- Finance Analysts
- Fraud Investigation Team
- Business Analysts
- Executive Leadership

---

## Features

### Transaction Monitoring

Monitor payment success and failure rates in real time.

### Revenue Reconciliation

Compare processed transactions against settlement records.

### Chargeback Analytics

Track disputes, merchant risk and chargeback trends.

### Data Quality Validation

Identify missing, duplicate and invalid operational records.

### Executive Reporting

Generate daily KPIs and operational dashboards.

### AI Investigation Assistant

Provide natural language explanations for operational anomalies.
