# RiskOps AI Platform

RiskOps AI Platform is an internal analytics solution designed to support Risk Operations teams with transaction monitoring, revenue reconciliation, chargeback intelligence, data quality validation and executive reporting.

The platform consolidates operational and financial data into a unified analytics layer, enabling analysts and business stakeholders to investigate anomalies, monitor key performance indicators and accelerate operational decision-making.

## Core Capabilities

* Transaction Intelligence
* Revenue Reconciliation
* Chargeback Analytics
* Data Quality Monitoring
* Executive KPI Reporting
* AI-Powered Investigation Assistant
